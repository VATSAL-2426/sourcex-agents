Module Detail

How It Works

Every module. Every trigger.
Every outcome.

Module 01
Missed-Call AI Agent
The moment a call is missed, an AI voice agent dials the patient back. It identifies itself as calling from the clinic, captures the reason for their call, and either books the appointment directly into the EMR or routes to the appropriate location.
→ Patient booked. Revenue recovered. No staff involved.
<60s
Callback time after missed call
Module 02
No-Show Recovery Engine
The moment a no-show is registered in the EMR, a recovery sequence fires: SMS first, then voice follow-up if no response within 30 minutes. Rebook logic attempts to fill the slot before end of business. Repeat no-shows can trigger deposit requirements.
→ Slot rebooked same day. Empty chair becomes revenue.
30%+
No-show reduction target
Module 03
Patient Reactivation Engine
Once per month, the system pulls all patients inactive 60+ days, segments them by case type (back pain, post-surgical, MVA, etc.), and sends personalized PHIPA-compliant outreach. No mass blasts — relevant, timed, compliant.
→ Dormant revenue reactivated. Competitor didn't get there first.
500–1,500
Avg. inactive patients per multi-location clinic
