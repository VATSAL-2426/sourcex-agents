Section 02

The Proposed Solution

Three AI modules, fully built and managed by SOURCE X. Integrated with your existing EMR. PHIPA-compliant by design. You do not manage any part of this system — you receive a monthly report showing exactly how much revenue was recovered.

Module 01
Missed-Call AI Agent
The moment a call is missed at any of your locations, an AI voice agent calls the patient back within 60 seconds. It speaks naturally, identifies the clinic, captures the reason for calling, and books or re-routes the appointment directly into your EMR — without any staff involvement.
  • 24/7 operation — catches after-hours and peak-hour misses
  • Direct EMR booking integration (Juvonno / Jane / ClinicSense)
  • Cross-location routing logic for multi-site clinics
  • PHIPA-compliant call recording and consent handling
$6,000
standalone value
Module 02
No-Show Recovery Engine
The moment a no-show is registered in your EMR, a recovery sequence fires automatically. SMS outreach first, followed by a voice follow-up if no response within 30 minutes. Rebook logic attempts to fill the vacated slot before end of business. Repeat no-show patients can trigger deposit requirements.
  • Fires within minutes of no-show event in EMR
  • SMS + voice dual-channel recovery sequence
  • Same-day rebooking logic — slot recovered before close
  • Repeat no-show deposit trigger (configurable)
$3,500
standalone value
Module 03
Patient Reactivation Engine
Once per month, the system pulls all patients who have not booked in 60+ days, segments them by case type, and sends personalized PHIPA-compliant outreach. Not mass blasts — relevant, timed, compliant messaging that brings dormant patients back before a competitor reaches them first.
  • Monthly automated pull of all 60+ day inactive patients
  • Segmented by case type: back, neck, post-op, MVA, general
  • PHIPA-compliant by design — consent-managed outreach
  • Booking links routed back to your EMR
$3,000
standalone value
